The process of establishing or establishing a higher education institution is not as easy as one might think, because the establishment of such an institution requires careful calculations and intensive study of various aspects of capabilities, opportunities, challenges and obstacles that may occur both at the time of establishment and during the establishment process.

To establish a university, it is necessary to have an organizing body in the form of a legal entity, in the form of a foundation that is used as a forum to protect the interests of the higher education institution it fosters, namely the University of Banten Jaya which is domiciled in Serang.

Banten Jaya University is one of the private universities in Indonesia in the form of a university, managed by the Higher Education and is included in Kopertis Region 4.

How to register here

Online: Go to the SPMB website banten jaya university Click the Register button to create an account by entering personal data, make payments at Bank BJB or make payments directly to campus 1 or 2 Banten Jaya university, Accounts are automatically activated after registration.

Offline: Come to campus 1 of Banten Jaya university which is located on Jl. Ciwaru, Serang City, Banten, Enter the PBM section of the University of Banten Jaya and further information will be explained by the PMB Section of the Banten Jaya University.
